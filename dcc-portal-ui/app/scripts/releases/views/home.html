About Us

The OHSU Knight Cancer Institute Data Portal provides tools for visualizing, querying, and downloading molecular and clinical data from OHSU projects together with large-scale public datasets.

The Data Portal is developed and maintained by the OHSU Computational Biology team in collaboration with Intel and the Ontario Institute for Cancer Research.

The Portal currently contains molecular data on over 16,000 samples from the International Cancer Genome Consortium, and is being actively updated to incorporate additional data from OHSU projects. For questions or requests please contact margolin@ohsu.edu.

The Collaborative Cancer Cloud (CCC) is a project led by Intel and OHSU to enable large-scale molecular and clinical data sharing and analysis through an interoperable distributed network of compute centers. The Data Portal is built on the CCC platform, which provides core technical capabilities for security, federation, and scalabilty.
